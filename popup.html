Expand Image Formats

Insert unsupported image formats into your Google Docs!

Simply drag and drop the image into Google Docs!

Usage Instructions

Google Document(Docs) / Presentation(Slides)

Drag and drop the image you want to insert into the document.

Google Spreadsheets(Sheets)

Unfortunately, drag and drop does not work

Copy the image you want to insert, then click on the cell and paste the image

Supported Image Format

'.webp', '.bmp', '.svg', '.avif'
